Summary

Pepar Hugo is an data scientist with over 6 years experience and has a background in call centers, financial forecasting and variance analysis, sales compensation, health analytics and map data. He has worked with Python, Docker, SQL, R, NoSQL, APIs and Apache Spark using local, on-premise and cloud solutions.

He has a proven track record of using data, analysis, machine learning and deep learning to make business decisions and discover new insights. His analysis has been used to implement new marketing promotions, workforce management changes and health data reporting. He's given presentations on deeping learning and computer vision proof of concept projects to senior officials in the Saskatchewan Healthcare System. He has used app event tracking to run user engagement A/B tests to make notification, product and feature recommendations to product and computer vision departments.

Pepar is passionate about data and searching for hidden value. He loves being presented unique challenges and problems to collaborate with collegeaus to provide them with a solution that fits their needs. He participates in the local Data for Good group in the Regina area.
